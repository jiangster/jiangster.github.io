Logos

July 2014 / Branding



Here are some logos I designed for various projects.

REAP Waterloo

REAP stands for Research Entrepreneurs Accelerating Prosperity (check them out here). REAP is a digital research lab looking for creative ways to combine mind-blowing technology with entrepreneurship, as seen in the video below.

One of REAP's latest projects is the Avavox - an AI display made from Christie Digital Microtiles and a GestureTek Cube. I had a pleasure of creating a logo for the platform to reflect the boundless technological innovation and the essense of fluidity.




University of Waterloo Breakers Club (UWB)

A logo I created with a friend for the club apparel. Breakdancer with boombox.



Kyudan

A work in progress logo I created for YouTube speedcuber Kyudan. Kyudan creates art out of Rubik's cubes.

November 2014 - ongoing / Game Design

Project: "The Leone Ranger"

For our Global Media Project, my team of 6 chose to develop an educational adventure game aimed at teaching young children in West Africa about the Ebola virus, safe sanitation practices and preventative measures in response to the Ebola outbreak. We designed it with the understanding that such a heavy topic is difficult to address in a game, and with careful planning and research we were able to integrate a program of knowledge into a channel that can be appropriately delivered to educate the young audience.

Development & Design

The Leone Ranger is currently being developed for the iOS programmed through GameSalad.

The game is an adventure platformer that features educational dialogue, mini games, and fun/light graphics. I designed the game art to accurately portray the style, culture, and environment of Sierra Leone, with all sprites crafted in a pixel art style.




Example gameplay on the iPhone 6. The game is set to come out early December. Stay tuned!

Project Brief

For our Global Media Project, I chose to develop an educational platformer game with 5 classmates, aimed at teaching young children in West Africa about the Ebola virus, safe sanitation practices and preventative measures in response to the Ebola outbreak. We designed it with the understanding that such a heavy topic is difficult to address in a game. With careful planning and research, we were able to integrate a program of knowledge into a channel that can appropriately reach a young audience.



Development & Design

The Leone Ranger is an adventure platformer that features educational dialogue, mini games, and fun/light graphics. I designed the game art to accurately portray the style, culture, and environment of Sierra Leone, with all sprites crafted in a pixel art style.

Space Junk

April 2013 / Game Design

Project

One of the coolest things about my campus in Stratford is this three-storey Christie MicroTile display. Myself and 3 fellow Global Business and Digital Arts students were given the task of creating an interative game playable on this wall. We called our piece Space Junk.

The Game

Space Junk is a child-friendly shooter game with the aim of promoting a clean environment. Players play as Tommy Terra - a boy concerned with the environment - to destroy junk falling from the sky. I was responsible for the conceptualization of the game and character design.

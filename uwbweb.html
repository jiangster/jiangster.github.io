UWB Website

UX&UI Design

Project

The University of Waterloo is known for its breakdance scene. UW Breakers is the student-run breakdance club that hosts some of the biggest events in Canada, and it was about time the club had a website.

Development

As a member of the club myself, I took on this website project at the beginning of the year being responsible for both the design and programming. It was a great learning experience in terms of JavaScript, Jquery. and applying minimalism as I had little coding experience prior. You can take a look at the work in progress here!
